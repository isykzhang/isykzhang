Biography

Yongpan Zou is currently an Assistant Professor in the Smart Sensing and Mobile Sensing Lab (SSMC), belonging to the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering of Shenzhen University, since July 2017. Prior to this, he was a Postdoc in University of Macau since Feburary 2017. He obtained his Ph.D in 2017 in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Professor Lionel M. Ni. In 2013, he received his B.E in School of Chemical Engineering and Technology, Xi'an Jiaotong University.

Research Interests

Wearable and Mobile Computing, Human-computer Interaction, Ubiquitous computing

Projects

 

IWRS

The Indoor WiFi Radar Sense (IWRS) project is a system to detect, localize, and identify human motions and the environmental contexts using WiFi signals, such as human detection, line-of-sight condition identification, breath detection and lips reading.
